PUBG ML SOLO OPEN

PUBG ML SOLO OPEN will be held on Saturday 26 of November, starting at 1pm. Entry fee will be Rs500 per player. The tournament will have prize pool money of Rs30000. The format for the tournaments as well as prize distribution is below.

Format

There will be two group created randomly each containing 100 payers. Each groups will have only half of its participants move on through the upper brackets while the rest still have a chance through the lower bracet. As we move on only the top half of the 100 player will move on and the rest will be eliminted. Below is a visual reprentation of the bracket.

Each round consists of 5 games that will be played where players will get points for elimination and placements. 50 players with the most points moves through the upper brackets and the rest goes down to the lowers.
